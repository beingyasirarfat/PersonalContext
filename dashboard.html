1. Part of Speech: noun
1.1. Definition: Something which lasts for a short period of time. Synonyms: ephemeron
2. Part of Speech: adjective
2.1. Definition: Lasting for a short period of time. Synonyms: evanescent, fleeting, momentary, short, short-lived, temporary, transitory, volatile Antonyms: eternal, everlasting, permanent, timeless
2.2. Definition: Existing for only one day, as with some flowers, insects, and diseases.
2.3. Definition: (of a body of water) Usually dry, but filling with water for brief periods during and after precipitation.
